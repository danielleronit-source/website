The Pattern

It makes sense why you reach.

The real reason you reach isn't weakness. What you're reaching for is actually giving you something.

A drink helps you unwind. A smoke gives you a break. A person gives you safety or validation. Nicotine gives you focus. Scrolling gives you escape.

These things make sense. They're meeting a real need.

And when something meets a real need, your system will keep reaching for it until it learns another way.

In this 75-minute workshop, we're doing two things:

First, we're uncovering what you're actually getting from the substances, people, or habits you reach for. Once you understand what need is being met, the pattern starts to make sense.

Second, I'm going to teach you a powerful daily tool that helps you see all the ways this pattern has been serving you, while also helping you begin to get those needs met from a different source.

That's where the real shift starts. You begin to understand what you need, change your relationship with the thing you reach for, and build a new relationship with yourself.

The Method

Three pillars of freedom from the inside

01

Uncover

Uncover how the substance, person, or habit has been serving you — what it has been giving you, the real need it has been meeting, and the deeper emotional layers and unconscious beliefs underneath the reaching. Once you see the pattern clearly, it starts to make sense.

02

Release

Go to the root of where the pain, pattern, or block actually lives — and release it at the level of the nervous system. This is like a deep cleaning of the system: the unconscious beliefs, unresolved emotions, and automatic patterns that keep you reaching begin to soften.

03

Rebuild

Build a new relationship with yourself and with the things you reach for. Rewire your nervous system so you're no longer ruled by old patterns and emotions, and calm can become your natural state. This is where you begin to feel more agency and choice in your life.
