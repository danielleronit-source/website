Meet Danielle

I've been

I grew up in California, and at 21 I moved to Israel — knowing how to read and write Hebrew, but barely able to speak it.

"I'd smile politely because I hadn't caught what they said. I was translating every sentence in my head. Sound familiar?"

The fix wasn't more grammar. It was understanding that the block isn't about knowledge — it's about fear. Years later, living as a digital nomad in Thailand, I started teaching English differently. Not just language — but confidence, presence, and the tools to rewire the nervous system response that makes you freeze.
